A Letter To Dad ❤️

Dear Dad,

Thank you for every sacrifice you made for us.

Every sleepless night, every hard day, and every challenge you faced was all to give us a better life

Your constant support, belief in us, and encouragement to chase our dreams mean more than words can express.

You taught us strength when life became difficult, kindness when the world needed compassion, and courage when we felt afraid.

Every achievement we celebrate today carries a piece of your hard work and love.

No matter how old we become, we will always be your daughters.

Thank you for being our first hero, our guide, our protector, and our biggest inspiration.

Happy Father's Day ❤️

With Love,
Your Daughters ❤️
